Who We Are

A Legacy of Quranic Knowledge

15 Years of Guiding the Next Generation

Established in 2011, Darul Quran Academy has been a beacon of spiritual enlightenment and authentic Islamic teachings in Thayineri, Payyannur. Over the last decade and a half, our institution has focused on cultivating a comprehensive understanding of the Holy Quran, Islamic jurisprudence (Fikh), Tajweed, and character development.

We are committed to providing a sanctuary of learning where students of all ages can connect deeply with the Quranic sciences while staying grounded in contemporary academic knowledge. Our campus fosters brotherhood, piety, and leadership skills required to serve society.

Our Vision

To be an exemplary global center of Islamic and modern educational excellence that builds virtuous individuals.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality memorization, recitation, and understanding of the Holy Quran integrated with ethical guidelines.

Our Curriculum

Educational Programs

Hifdh Al-Quran

A structured memorization course designed to help students memorize the entire Quran with speed, retention, and deep spiritual discipline under expert Huffaz guidance.

  • Daily Revision (Sabaq, Sabqi, Amfta)
  • Personalized Pace Scheduling
  • Regular Evaluation Forums
Inquire Program

Tajweed & Qira'at

Focuses on correct articulation, rules of recitation, and the melodic art of Qira'at, ensuring students recite the Quran exactly as it was revealed.

  • Makharij (Mouth Points) training
  • Rules of Waqf and Ibtida'
  • Practical application classes
Inquire Program

Islamic & Shariah Studies

Provides basic to advanced understanding of Islamic jurisprudence (Fikh), Hadith, Aqeedah (theology), and Islamic History (Seerah) to guide daily life.

  • Authentic books and references
  • Character building (Akhlaq)
  • Modern contextual analysis
Inquire Program

Integrated Academic Stream

Enabling student-huffaz to continue their formal school education (English, Mathematics, Science, Social Sciences) alongside their religious studies.

  • Certified curriculum syllabus
  • Expert academic subject teachers
  • Balancing science and faith

Admission Process & Inquiry

How to Enroll

Admissions are open throughout the year. We prioritize students based on moral foundation, basic Arabic reading capacity, and interview performances.

01

Online Inquiry / Visit

Fill out the online inquiry form here or visit our campus at Thayineri directly for registration details.

02

Interview & Evaluation

Prospective students will undergo a basic assessment of Arabic letters (Tajweed) and an interview with Hafiz Shameer Saadi.

03

Admission Confirmation

Upon successful verification of background documents, admission will be confirmed, and boarding hostel slots will be allocated.
